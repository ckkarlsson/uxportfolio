Background

“Fly Over the World” is a booking platform for sightseeing flights. They operate in various cities across the world like Amsterdam, Stockholm, Dubai, Venice, Sidney, etc. They offer sightseeing flights in small airplanes, helicopters and hot air balloons. Fly Over the World brings together the public and the flight operator via their booking platform. Their aim is that all their guests have a once in a lifetime experience in which the customer can enjoy the flight and they take care of the rest.

The current front page on a desktop

The Challenge

Fly Over the World started as a small company in the Netherlands, and their website was designed according to their scale of operations. As they grew internationally over the years, so did they try to adapt their platform as well. This organic growth has led to a rather complex structure, of which it’s not always clear to the user what the complete offer of the company is. They struggle with creating a unified and smooth user experience for their customers.

Fly Over the World is currently working on their 3rd overhaul of their online platform, as they have already outgrown the previous two versions. They realize the need to improve their overall user experience since the competition in the flight industry is fierce. They see their biggest challenges to create a simple and unified platform and a clear and simple end-to-end journey for their customers.

Project Goals

1. Design the user interaction with the new Fly Over The World booking platform.

2. Design a system-level perspective of the complete user flow and wireframes of selected service phases that are ready for the development of the new booking platform.
